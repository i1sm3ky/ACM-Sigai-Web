Medical Health Buddy

Medical Health Buddy provides a range of features aimed at enhancing healthcare insights:

i. Disease Prediction:
Users input their symptoms, and the TensorFlow Keras model predicts the top 4 potential diseases with an accuracy exceeding 85%. Trained on a dataset of over 5000 patient records, this feature aids in early disease identification.

ii. Lab Report Analysis:
By entering key information from lab reports, users receive predictions on potential liver disease, heart disease, or diabetes. Three specialized models, each trained on datasets of around 1000 patient records with over 90% accuracy, contribute to this insightful analysis.

- Diabetes Prediction:
Trained on medical information and lab analyses of Iraqi patients, sourced from Medical City Hospital and the Specialized Center for Endocrinology and Diabetes (Al-Kindi Teaching Hospital).

- Heart Disease Prediction:
Trained on a dataset of about 300 patients, utilizing cardiac catheterization as the gold standard for classifying individuals with or without heart disease.

- Liver Disease Prediction:
Trained on a dataset of approximately 600 patients from the north-eastern region of Andhra Pradesh, India, this model aids in predicting the likelihood of liver disease based on lab analyses.

Medical Health Buddy leverages these models to empower users with valuable health insights, fostering early detection and informed decision-making.

Paralegal AI

Paralegal AI is a legal support tool powered by artificial intelligence, focused on simplifying document generation and editing processes. Its user-friendly chat-based interface provides clear explanations of legal implications for document changes, while document summarization eliminates complex legal jargon for improved understanding. Prioritizing user privacy, Paralegal AI employs a private LLM model and includes OCR for extracting text from scanned documents. With multilingual support and secure cloud-based storage for documents, it ensures accessibility and efficiency. The platform also enables scheduling consultations with legal experts and incorporates speech-to-text functionality for enhanced usability. In essence, Paralegal AI is a comprehensive solution for managing, understanding, and communicating legal documents.

Analysis of End-to-End Encryption Research: Developments, Limitations and Future Directions

This paper undertakes a comprehensive, interdisciplinary review of recent end-to-end encryption (E2EE) research. It provides critical analysis of E2EE techniques, applications, policy debates, user perspectives, and cross-disciplinary challenges. The review synthesizes technical developments in cryptography while also drilling into complex socio-ethical dimensions of real-world E2EE deployment. It highlights critical knowledge gaps warranting further research and distils core insights across the literature. The paper emphasizes the urgent need for continued collaboration between computer science, law, policy, social science, and design fields to advance E2EE solutions that holistically balance security, privacy, usability, lawful access, and societal trust.

Advanced ML and Deep learning for Disease Diagnosis: Predictive Symptom Analysis

This disease prediction system utilizes Multinomial Naive Bayes, Random Forest, K-Nearest Neighbour, Logistic Regression, Support Vector Machine, Decision Tree, Multilayer Perceptron, Voting classifier and Deep learning sequential models to accurately predict diseases based on observed symptoms. The system incorporates data preprocessing techniques, model training using multiple algorithms, evaluation, and disease prediction. The effectiveness of the system is evaluated using various evaluation metrics, providing insights into the accuracy and reliability of the predictions. The results demonstrate the potential of the system in assisting medical professionals in diagnosing diseases and making informed decisions.
